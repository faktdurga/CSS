छत्रपती शिवाजी महाराज

इंद जिमि जृंभपर। बाढब सअंबपर ।। रावण सदंभपर। रघुकुलराज है ।। पवन बारिबाहपर। संभू रतिनाहपर ।। जो सहसबाहपर। रामद्विजराज है ।। दावा दुमदंडपर। चीता मृग झंडपर ।। भूषण वितुंडपर। जैसे मृगराज है ।। तेज तम अंशपर। कन्नजिमि कंसपर ।। जो म्लेंछ वंशपर। शेर शिवराज है ।। शेर शिवराज है।।

English Translation: Like lord Indra against the demons, Like underwater volcanic fire over the ocean Like lord Ram of the raghus clan against boastful ravana, As strong wind scatters the clouds of rain As lord Siva destroyed rati’s husband(kaamdev), and Like parsurama destroyed sahastraarjuna – Like fire which burns woods, and a Cheetha on herd of deer , Like lion on hogs, so says Bhushan. Like a ray of light in darkness, Like boy Krishna upon kansa, So has descended king Shivaji ,the Tiger. upon the clan of mlecchas(barbarians)
